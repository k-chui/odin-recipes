Lasagna

Description

This is the best lasagna ever. I didn't like lasagna before I tried this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 1 container of ricotta cheese
  • 1 bag of shredded mozarella

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water.
  2. Layer the ingredients in an oven safe tray, topping with mozarella.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 350ÂºF for 45 minutes.
  4. Cut and serve.
