Steak

Description

This steak is so juicy and flavourful.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add steak.
  3. Sear steak untouched for 3 minutes, then flip and sear for another 3 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and season with black pepper.
